OUR STORY
Our Story
A Journey of Faith
The Urdu Hindi Christian Fellowship began with a simple vision: to create a place where
 Urdu and Hindi-speaking believers could worship God in their heart language while building
 meaningful relationships with fellow Christians.
What started as a small prayer group has grown into a vibrant community of faith,
 welcoming people from diverse backgrounds who share a common love for Christ.
 Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness and the dedication of our members.
Today, UHCF stands as a testament to God's grace, offering worship services,
 Bible studies, youth programs, and community outreach initiatives that touch lives
 both locally and beyond.

OUR VISION
Our Vision
To be a beacon of hope and love, bringing the message of Jesus Christ to Urdu and Hindi
 speaking communities worldwide, nurturing disciples who transform their families,
 workplaces, and neighborhoods with God's love.

WHAT WE BELIEVE
What We Believe
Our Core Values
These values guide everything we do and shape who we are as a community.
Love
We are committed to loving God and loving others as ourselves, creating a community where everyone feels valued and accepted.
Faith
We stand firm in our faith in Jesus Christ, teaching and living out biblical principles in our daily lives.
Community
We believe in the power of fellowship, supporting each other through life's joys and challenges.
Cultural Heritage
We celebrate our Urdu and Hindi heritage while embracing diversity and welcoming all who seek God.

OUR MISSION
Our Mission
“To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ among Urdu and Hindi speaking people,
 equipping them to live out their faith and share God's love with the world.”

OUR JOURNEY
Our Journey
Milestones
2010 – Foundation
 UHCF was established with a small group of believers.
2013 – First Building
 We moved into our first dedicated worship space.
2016 – Youth Ministry
 Launched our vibrant youth and children's programs.
2019 – Online Presence
 Expanded our reach through digital ministry.
2023 – Growing Strong
 Continuing to grow and serve our community.

COMMON QUESTIONS
FAQs

What should I wear to service?
Come as you are! We have no dress code. Most people dress casually.

Is there parking available?
Yes, we have free parking available for all visitors.

Do you have programs for children?
Yes! We have age-appropriate programs during Sunday service for kids ages 3–12.

What languages are services in?
Services are conducted in Urdu and Hindi with some English elements.
